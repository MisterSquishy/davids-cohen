Personalized Legal Services

Davids & Cohen is a law firm with offices in Wellesley, Massachusetts; Hartford, Connecticut; Providence, Rhode Island; and Tampa, Florida. The firm has built its reputation by providing high quality, personalized legal services at reasonable rates. The firm represents both individual and corporate clients. The services include consultations, negotiations and litigation, including trials and appeals, on employment, business, contract, personal injury, real estate and insurance issues.

Each attorney strives to handle every matter as if it were the highest priority for that client and works hard to provide creative, straightforward and excellent results. The firm does not lose sight of the overriding objective in every matter our clients entrust to us, large or small; to bring about the best possible result in the most efficient manner achievable.

Davids & Cohen devotes itself to providing its clients with the expertise, value, commitment and results they deserve in a personal, creative and efficient way.
